MISSION STATEMENT

“Where students go to connect, brainstorm & share their entrepreneurial mindset”

Innoblue is a safe place for people to find their passions, being given the opportunities by our organization to be exposed to countless different entrepreneurial careers.

A COMMUNITY OF SHARED PRIDE AND SUCCESS

Here, passion is met with collaboration in an environment that is rooted in shared pride for the accomplishments of our fellow students, faculty, staff, and alumni.

With a connected entrepreneurial community caring for hundreds of members and countless connected alumni, cheering each other on to build a culture committed to building the best professional and motivated personalities.

And that culture is diverse—our community is made up of students from all walks of life and countless countries around the world. Even after graduation, our community continues to share expertise and support. Our global network of hundreds of alumni is available to students when they want advice on job networking, mentoring opportunities, and what to expect in the future.
